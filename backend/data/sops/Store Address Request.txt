NAME: Store Address Request
SUMMARY: Guidance on providing the physical warehouse address. Priority is given to bulk orders, as the warehouse is not a standard retail location.
STEPS:
1. Ask if the customer is requesting the address for a bulk order.
2. If NOT a bulk order, inform the customer that we do not typically operate as a retail storefront from the warehouse.
3. If the customer is adamant about visiting to purchase or for a bulk order, provide the Hyderabad warehouse address.
4. Address details: 4th Floor, Al Ameen Plaza Complex, Balanagar, Hyderabad, Telangana – 500037.
5. Send the 'Store Address detail' email template to the customer.
