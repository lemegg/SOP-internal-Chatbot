NAME: Order Status Inquiry
SUMMARY: Assists in responding to customers asking about the status or delivery timeframe of their orders. It handles orders in transit and those out for delivery.
STEPS:
1. Check the current status of the order on the tracking portal or Woo-commerce.
2. If the order is in transit for less than 15 days, ask the customer to wait for a few more days.
3. If the order is marked as "Out for Delivery", inform the customer that it will reach them shortly.
4. Advise the customer to keep their phone handy and ensure someone is available at the delivery address.
5. Send the corresponding email template (Order in Transit or Out for Delivery) to the customer.
