NAME: TAOS Point Issues
SUMMARY: Handle customer queries regarding TAOS point redemption problems or expired points. This procedure covers re-login glitches and issuing equivalent coupons when points are unusable.
STEPS:
1. Ask the customer to log out and log back in to resolve potential technical glitches.
2. If the problem persists after re-login, offer a coupon of the same value as the TAOS points.
3. Check TAOS point validity; points expire 6 months from the credit date.
4. If points are expired, politely inform the customer about the 6-month validity policy.
5. If points are NOT expired but still unusable, check Woo-commerce settings for errors.
6. If the issue cannot be resolved on Woo-commerce, issue an equivalent coupon valid for one month.
7. Send the appropriate email template (Problem in Redeeming, Expired, or Coupon Issued) to the customer.
