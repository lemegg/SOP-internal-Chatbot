NAME: Wrong Shipping Address
SUMMARY: Procedure for when a customer reports they placed an order with an incorrect or incomplete shipping address. Covers both pre-shipment corrections and post-shipment redirections.
STEPS:
1. Verify if the order has already been shipped by checking the status in Woo-commerce/Superapp.
2. IF NOT SHIPPED: Ask the customer for the full correct address including landmarks and pincode.
3. Update the shipping address in the system immediately.
4. IF ALREADY SHIPPED: Contact the courier partner to check if address redirection is possible.
5. Inform the customer that redirection may cause a 2-3 day delivery delay.
6. If redirection is NOT possible, wait for the order to RTO (Return to Origin) and then process a reshipment to the correct address.
7. Send the 'Address Correction Confirmation' email to the customer.
